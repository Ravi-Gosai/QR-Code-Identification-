About QR Code Generator

The QR Code-Based Emergency Contact Information System is a project designed to enhance safety and expedite the process of disseminating critical information to the family and authorities in the event of an accident or emergency involving a person. This innovative system leverages QR (Quick Response) codes to store and retrieve essential details such as the individual's name, phone number, and vehicle registration number.


In this project, users can input their personal information into a secure database. This database generates a unique QR code for each user, containing their vital details. The QR code can be printed and affixed to a visible location on the user's vehicle or kept in a personal wallet.


This project represents a fusion of modern technology, user safety, and efficient emergency response mechanisms. It serves as a valuable tool for promoting public safety and well-being.
